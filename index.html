About me

Hello Everyone! My name is Marc Ramirez, you may know me online as GNamimates. Born and raised in the Philippines, I'm a -year-old Computer Science student with a passion for animation and game development. I love bringing ideas to life, whether it's through animating in Blender or crafting games and tools in the Godot engine.

In my free time, you'll usually find me coding it's more than a hobby, it's my world. While people often find me a bit unconventional, I've been lucky to meet others who match my energy and join me on this creative journey.

Over the years, I've honed my skills to the point where I've served as a teacher's assistant for classes in architecture design, animation, and programming. My goal is to continue creating, learning, and inspiring others along the way.

Lastly, I cannot describe the deep and unending love I have for my parents. They've sacrificed so much to give me the time and energy to pursue my passions, even if what I do on my laptop remains a mystery to them. If you're reading this, thank you for believing in me and for all the sacrifices you've made you mean everything to me.
